Methodology

At the heart of this algorithm is a model that has already performed classification using Logistic Regression on data that has been collected from a sample of one-and-half lakh individuals and learnt the relationship between the heights, weights, ages, cholestrol levels, haemoglobin, lipids and lipoproteins and the corresponding condition of being a smoker or a non-smoker.

The algorithm on this website does not perform classification on the fly. The data entered via this form is fed to the model which analyses it and calculates whether a person is likely a smoker or not.
